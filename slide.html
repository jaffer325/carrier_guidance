Which field to choose?

The biggest decision post class 12 is picking the right stream. Picking the right stream is extremely important as everything that happens thereafter will depend on it. The Science stream offers you many lucrative career options such as engineering, medical, IT and computer science and even research in a variety of domains. Commerce stream can lead you to some of the most lucrative and high paying jobs such as Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Accountants, Investment Banking and Financial Advisors. An Arts student can opt for career options like Journalism, Literature, Social Work, Teaching and many others.



ARTS

Humanities or what is popularly known as the Arts stream is traditionally the least preferred option among Class 10 students but this perception is changing with time. An Arts student can opt for career options like Journalism, Literature, Social Work, Teaching and many others.

ENGINEERING

Engineering offers a wide range of career paths, including fields like mechanical, civil, electrical, and computer engineering. It focuses on solving real-world problems through innovation and technology, providing job stability and opportunities for growth in various industries.

MEDICAL

The Medical field offers diverse career opportunities such as doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and researchers. With the growing global demand for healthcare professionals, it provides excellent job stability and career growth. This field allows individuals to make a meaningful impact on society while pursuing a rewarding and fulfilling career.
